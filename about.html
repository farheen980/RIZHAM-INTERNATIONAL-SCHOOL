Vision

In an era where continuous learning is essential, RIS builds an education system that provides strong, transferable skills and allows learners to return when needed.

RIS empowers students to become RIZHAMian's, Indians, and Global Citizens.

Objectives

  • Providing quality education accessible to everyone.
  • Embracing Multiple Intelligence and diverse learning styles.
  • Learning-centered approach.
  • Developing life skills.
  • Providing conditions to learn and think independently.

Rules & Regulations

  • Students must attend school regularly and maintain at least 85% attendance.
  • Proper school uniform must be worn daily with neat appearance.
  • Respect towards teachers, staff, and fellow students is compulsory.
  • Use of mobile phones inside school campus is strictly prohibited.
  • Students must arrive on time; late coming will not be encouraged.
  • Maintain discipline in classroom, assembly, and school premises.
  • Any form of bullying or misbehavior will lead to strict action.
  • Care must be taken to keep school property clean and safe.
  • Homework and assignments must be completed on time.
  • Participation in school activities is encouraged for overall development.

Discipline + Respect + Learning = RIS Excellence
